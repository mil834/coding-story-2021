It has been almost 20 years since the first Spider-Man movie was released. With the new film being released in a few weeks, let's take a trip down memory lane.

2002

Young Peter Parker (how it all began, with Tobey Maguire)

2004

Peter Parker development of powers and fighting Octavius (Tobey Maguire)

2007

Peter Parker fighting new enemies (Tobey Maguire)

2012

Peter Parker discovers his new abilities, tracks down his father's killer (Andrew Garfield)

2014

Peter Parker is tracked down by Max Dillon, who needs Peter's blood to save him. However, Peter ends up fighting Dillon, now known as Electro.

2017

Peter Parker gets brought into the Avengers by Tony Stark (Iron Man) and learns how to control and harness his powers (Tom Holland).

2019

Peter battles the Elementals (water, earth, fire, air) and builds his relationship with MJ (Tom Holland).

2021

Peter Parker gets help from Doctor Strange to go back in time and make it so that nobody knows he's Spider-Man, and it causes absolute chaos (Tom Holland).
